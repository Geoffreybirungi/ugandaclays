Building Solutions

UCL products are all baked to approximately 1000 degrees centigrade and are thus fire-resistant. They are also sound and water proof and act as good heat insulators. These qualities are ensured by quality assurance testing constantly done throughout the production process. In these tests they are checked for linear shrinkage and size (every two hours), their moisture content before and after baking, water absorption, as well as composition of salts.

ISO Certification

Periodic tests are made on samples sent to Uganda National Bureau of Standards and Central Materials Laboratory. All these tests ensure that all products sold are of unmatched quality, a cornerstone of UCL since obtaining ISO 9001:2000 certification in 2003. We have completed the upgrade to the ISO 9001:2008 standard.We are pleased to announce that we are now ISO 9001:2008 certified since March 2014

Technical Advisory Services

Uganda Clays has a fully-fledged technical team that will provide you with all the information that you may require if you are interested in learning more about the options that we offer in the construction industry.


Whether you have a general inquiry that can be serviced via email or telephone, through to complex site visits, our dedicated technical team is here to help.

      Advice on general enquiries made verbally, by phone or by email.

      Specification of the product required for particular parts/sections/place.

      After-sales technical service to all our esteemed customers.

Estimates for quantities required for a particular length or square area.

For example;

      How many tiles go on a particular square are of a roof.

      How many Max Spans go on a particular square area of a floor.

      All you need to avail the team is an architectural or structural plan for the building and or onsite visit of the building or construction in question.

Basic skills training on the application of our products.

      In case of new products and or new areas with no skilled manpower in the site location, UCL technical team disseminates application skills to the builders at site.
